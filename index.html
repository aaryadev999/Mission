Our Environment!

Our environment is not in the best condition. We humans have polluted our environment beyond repair! Here are some steps we can follow to save the environment from further damage:


Reduce, Reuse and Recycle
Volunteer for cleanups in your community
Conserve water
Shop wisely
Plant a tree

Our environment is also affected by Air Pollution, Water Pollution, Noise Pollution and Land Pollution. if we bring our focus on Land Pollution, we find that Land Pollution is mostly caused by cutting trees. It can also be caused by construction of huge buildings taking up space and causing lack of greenery. To solve this problem we can plant more trees, and construct lesser buildings. Proper disposal of plastics will also help.

Next we will be bringing our focus on Water Pollution.
Water Pollution is mostly caused by oil-leaks from ships, and humans throwing plastic in the water. This harms the marine animals. To fix this problem we can start creating eco-friendly ships, and we can stop throwing plastic in the water.
Next, we have Air Pollution.
Air Pollution is mostly caused by Big Truck, Cars, and Big Factories. To stop this we can control toxic factory smoke, use public transport, and avoid crackers.
Lastly we have Noise Pollution.
Noise Pollution is mostly caused by trafic, crackers, and construction sites. To stop noise Pollution, we can wear ear-plugs when construction work is going on, turning off appliances when not in use, and ban of crackers.
These were all the types of Pollutions and how we can solve the different issues.
